## Risk Assessment

### Risks to 98% Target

1. **Lab 01 gamification gap**: First impression for every student. No XP, no streaks, no milestones. Students may think the gamification system is absent until Lab 02. **Mitigation**: Either upgrade Lab 01 or add a brief note explaining the lighter gamification in the intro lab.

2. **Lab 05 monolithic localStorage**: A single corrupt STATE key wipes all 5 sections of progress. **Mitigation**: This is a development-quality concern, not user-visible unless they clear localStorage. Lower priority.

3. **Challenge Missions render as markdown**: The `/challenges` route uses module-viewer.html to render markdown. This works but offers no interactivity, no completion tracking, no XP. **Mitigation**: Acceptable for launch; dedicated page is a v2 enhancement.

4. **3 modules have no labs**: M06 (MCP Building), M08 (Meta-Prompting), M11 (Tech Stack) have no dedicated labs. **Mitigation**: These are architectural decisions from the 3-day course format. Not a gap for launch — documented as known limitation.
