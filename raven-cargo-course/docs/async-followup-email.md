# Async Follow-Up Email -- Raven Cargo AI Academy

**Subject:** Raven Cargo AI Academy -- Your Capstone Track Is Live

---

## Plain Text Version (with Markdown links)

Hi Team,

Thank you for two great days of training on March 24-25. The energy and questions from the group made it a genuinely productive session.

The async capstone phase is now open and runs through **April 17**. The platform has your next step: a production pipeline design tailored to your role at Raven Cargo.

**Your Capstone Track**
Pick the track that matches your role -- Technical, Accounting, or Business. Each track applies the same five-phase design framework to a real Raven Cargo problem.
[Choose Your Track](https://academy.raven-cargo.app/raven-cargo-course/async#role-tracks)

Three tracks, three capstones each:
- **Technical (T1--T3):** PR review pipeline, incident triage router, data quality monitor
- **Accounting (A1--A3):** invoice reconciliation, cost allocation reports, rate audit and compliance
- **Business (B1--B3):** carrier performance dashboard, customer exception communication, customs documentation

**The Five Design Phases**
Every capstone follows the same framework: Problem Scoping, Pipeline Architecture, Prompt and Skill Design, Safety and Permissions, Validation. Complete each phase before writing any configuration.
[View the Phases](https://academy.raven-cargo.app/raven-cargo-course/async#design-phases)

**3-Week Timeline**
- Week 1 (Mar 30 -- Apr 5): Pick your track. Complete Problem Scoping and Architecture.
- Week 2 (Apr 6 -- Apr 12): Complete Prompt Design and Safety. Submit a concept challenge.
- Week 3 (Apr 13 -- Apr 17): Complete Validation. Submit your capstone. Fill out the evaluation.

**Concept Challenges**
Eight Raven-specific challenges remain available on the Challenges page, split by non-technical (C1--C4) and technical (C5--C8).
[View Challenges](https://academy.raven-cargo.app/raven-cargo-course/challenges#concept-challenges)

**Submissions**
Submit your capstone deliverables and challenge artifacts here.
[Submit Your Work](https://academy.raven-cargo.app/raven-cargo-course/submissions)

**Course Evaluation**
A short, confidential feedback form -- your input directly shapes future sessions.
[Complete the Evaluation](https://academy.raven-cargo.app/raven-cargo-course/evaluations)

---

My suggestion: go to the Async page, pick the track closest to your role, and read through the capstone that interests you most. Then start Phase 1 -- define the trigger and the success criterion. You will be surprised how quickly the rest follows.

If you have any questions, just reply to this email. I am happy to help.

Best regards,
Manu
